Almond Butter
$8
Almond Butter was the first peanut butter alternative that we started making. Peanut butter is great, but we wanted to share our Nut Butters with everyone, including people who are allergic to peanuts. Our Almond Butter has the same salty-sweet taste as our peanut butter as well as a creamy texture, but provides a peanut butter alternative to those who can't enjoy our Peanut Butter.
