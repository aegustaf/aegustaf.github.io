The Start of It All

Eric's Nut Butters is a family business that started with the Gustafson family's wish for no artificial ingredients in their favorite foods. The Gustafson family moves a lot and every time they move they must find their new go-to products in the grocery store because they change by region of the country. They had just moved to Charlotte and could not find the brand of peanut butter that they usually got that didn't have added ingredients. After trying option after option and feeling as though none of the peanut butters would ever hit the mark, Eric decided to try his hand at making his own peanut butter for the family. He took his food processor and a lot of peanuts and made peanut butter.

More Than Just Peanut Butter

Eric's Nut Butters aims to go where peanut butter never dared to go before....beyond the peanut. Eric is dedicated to providing his delicious butters to those who have peanut allergies and those who desire another kind of nut experience. With hard work and determination Eric and the Gustafsons have expanded beyond peanut butter and offer: almond butter, cashew butter, walnut butter, and Macadamia Nut butter.

How to Find Us

Eric's Nut Butters can be found at the Charlotte Farmers' Market every Saturday and Sunday from April to October. You can also navigate to our Connect page to place a custom nut butter order which can be available for pickup at the Charlotte Farmers' Market, YMCA Dowd Gym, or for pickup at another location.
