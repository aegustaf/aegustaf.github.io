Connect with Us Online
Stay up-to-date with Eric's Nut Butters and follow us on our social media platforms. We are currently on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter. Connect with In-Person
Come visit Eric's Nut Butters' stand at the Charlotte Market on Saturdays and Sundays from April to October. We will be selling our nut butters and any special or seasonal products that we have. Place an order online, come pick it up from our stand and roam around the rest of the Farmers' Market. Make sure to check our social medias to find out our hours. We can't wait to see you!
